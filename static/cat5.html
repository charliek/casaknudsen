TAXI
Taxis are plentiful and reasonably inexpensive around Playa. From Cancun airport
There are many services which will transport you in an air conditioned van from Cancun airport to Playa for around $40-$60 apiece round trip (depending on size of party) see www.playa.info
RENTAL CARS
We prefer to rent a car as it gives you a lot of flexibility, but it is by no means essential and is fairly expensive for a week when you take the insurance (don't forget, this is Mexico). The drive from Cancun is 40 miles on good 4-lane roads.

There are lots of rental car agencies in Playa for day trips if you want to take a van down and plan your excursions by the day.
DIRECTIONS:
Playa Del Carmen is located 30-40 minutes south of Cancun Airport on Hwy 307 a 4 lane highway. "Casa Knudsen" is located at the north end of town in Capri Condominiums, a nine unit complex at 32nd street and First Avenue. Roughly one block to the ocean to the east, and one block to Fifth Avenue the main walking street through town.
