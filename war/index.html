We are happy to share our tropical home in Playa del Carmen with each of you. It is a wonderful 2 bedroom condo new in 2005, just a block from the best beaches in the Yucatan and a block the other way from the main walking street through town, full of bars, restaurants and shops. We are located on the north end of Playa in the "Italian District ".

Playa del Carmen has all the things we had on our checklist before we bought here. Great bars and restaurants, excellent golf, snorkeling and water sports, great weather and beaches and the charm of a seaside city where no building can be more than 3 stories.

Our condo also has many appealing and modern features including air conditioning, washer and dryer, nice audio and video equipment with cable and dvd in the living room and cable tv in the master bedroom. Also, special touches like gallery quality mayan artwork and two king size beds.

The view of the Caribbean and the lights of Cozumel from our patio at night is also a nice reminder that you're on vacation!
